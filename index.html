The Problem

Paid logistics.
Going nowhere.

Amid rising fuel costs, every empty quick commerce return trip is an increasingly expensive missed opportunity. Millions of return trips daily — each one burning fuel, earning nothing. Meanwhile, India's EPR compliance burden on FMCG brands grows every year with no scalable collection solution.

⛽

Rising fuel costs

Every empty return kilometre costs more today than last year. Idle logistics is increasingly expensive to justify.

♻️

EPR compliance gap

FMCG brands face legally mandated recycling targets rising from 35% to 70% by 2028-29. Collection infrastructure doesn't exist at scale.

🏙️

Urban waste crisis

68% of quick commerce customers have no formal dry waste segregation facility within 500 metres of their home.

📦

Quick commerce packaging

Quick commerce generates approximately 400,000 tonnes of plastic packaging waste annually — a stream growing faster than any existing collection system.

The Solution

Three steps.
Zero extra fleet.

1

Customer opts in

Customer requests a BlinkBack collection bag through the quick commerce app. Rider delivers it on the next order — no separate trip needed.

2

Bag swap on return

When the bag is full, the rider swaps the filled bag for a new empty one on the next delivery to that zone. Ten seconds. Zero route change. ₹5 bonus credited instantly.

3

Dark store aggregates

Dark stores aggregate sorted waste by category. Recycling partners collect every 24-48 hours. Material sold. EPR certificates issued.
